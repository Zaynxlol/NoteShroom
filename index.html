HOW TO USE NOTESHROOM

  1. Click the Start button or press the microphone to begin recording
  2. Speak clearly into your microphone
  3. Click Stop when you're finished
  4. Your speech will be converted to text automatically
  5. Use the Save button to download your notes as a text file
